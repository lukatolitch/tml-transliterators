<cu-c>Sl#-nce zhe teche(t) na b"z(d)uh" v" d#en',
a v noshti po kie'nu nisko l#eti(t). Ne
#omocha(s), no tkmo .g#-.-(zh) #omyvae(t)se'
v okie'ne.</cu-c>

The sun proceeds through the air duringthe daytime and at night flies low over theocean. It does not immerse, but onlywashes itself three times in the ocean.

Source: Kulik (2016), p. 18.
